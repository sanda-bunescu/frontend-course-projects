Quiche

Description

This spinach mushroom quiche is a moist vegetarian quiche perfect for any meal. Feel free to add sausage if you choose. I usually make 2 large pies or several small ones at one time, freezing whatever is left.

Ingredients

  • 5 ounces fresh spinach
  • 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
  • Â½ yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 ounces crumbled feta cheese
  • Spices and seasonings
  • 1 9-inch deep dish prepared pie crust
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Gently combine spinach, mushrooms, onion, and feta cheese in a bowl. Spread mixture into prepared pie crust.
  3. Whisk eggs, milk, parsley, garlic, salt and black pepper in the bowl. Pour egg mixture evenly over filling.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly puffed and browned, 45 to 50 minutes.
